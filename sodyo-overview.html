Any Distance

  • The markers are scannable from much further distances than with other technologies, or
  • The marker can be smaller and take up less space for the same scanning distance.
  • Typical scanning distances are 100x the shortest side of the marker.
  • A marker that is 3cm on its shortest side can be scanned from 3m away, which in most cases is from across the room.
  • A marker that is 25cm on its shortest side can be scanned from 25m away, which would apply to digital signs and outdoor billboards.
  • In layman's terms, codes can be scanned within eye reach.

Multi-Marker Scanning

  • Any number of makers can be scanned at one time.
  • The limit is the number of markers that can fit in the scanner window.

Fast

  • Near instantaneous scanning time provides a superior user experience.

Robust

  • The technology is indifferent to changes in lighting making markers able to be scanned in bright and low lighting conditions
  • Wide scanning angle of 150 degrees

Any Media

  • Print, Digital, you name it. If you can put a marker on it, you can scan it.
  • Why is this important? Integrated advertising campaigns in real world interactive environments.
