Overview

RPC has integrated the Sodyo scanning technology into their mobile application. They currently use it during one TV show called EstÃºdio C, and the scanner is only enabled during the time that show is on the air. In order to launch the scanner, there is an "Interactivity" button that pops up on the app, the user simply pushes the button which launches the scanner and then scans the marker on screen.

RPC has put marketing resources around the launch of the technology. They are utilizing this as a fun and cool way to interact with a fun show. Along with that, they introduced a mascot called Clarinha who appears on screen in order to let the viewer know that a code will be appearing.  

They announced the new technology on all of their social media and their website. Before the first airing, they posted an infographic with step-by-step instructions explaining how to use the scanner and a video tutorial also explaining how to easy it is to use. There have been additional tutorials posted and aired on TV as well.
